Her Story

From Girl's Day to beloved actress - a journey we've been honored to witness

Why We Love Her

She debuted as Girl's Day's maknae in 2010, bringing sunshine into our lives with her infectious energy. From K-pop stages to becoming one of Korea's most cherished actresses, we've watched her grow and shine.

In 2014, she earned the title "Nation's Little Sister" on Real Men, and honestly, we all felt it. Then came Reply 1988 in 2015 - Deok-sun became a part of all of us. The show became a cultural phenomenon, and we couldn't have been prouder.

Her journey continues to inspire us. In 2025, she starred in Friendly Rivalry, once again proving her incredible range as an actress. We're so lucky to support someone who keeps challenging herself and growing.
