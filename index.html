My Story

From Girl's Day to acclaimed actress - celebrating 15 years in entertainment

About

Born June 9, 1994 in Gwangju, South Korea. I debuted as the youngest member of Girl's Day in 2010, embarking on a journey that would span music, television, and film.

In 2014, I earned the title "Nation's Little Sister" through the variety show Real Men. My breakthrough role came in 2015 with Reply 1988, which became one of the highest-rated Korean cable dramas in television history.

In 2016, I ranked third in Forbes Korea Power Celebrity list. Today, I continue to challenge myself with diverse roles, most recently starring in the acclaimed drama Friendly Rivalry in 2025.
